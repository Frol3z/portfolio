ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Gabriele Novello, a Computer Science graduate student based in Turin, Italy. I am currently completing my Master's degree at the University of Turin, with a focus on Computer Vision and Computer Graphics, and I plan to graduate in November 2026.


I'm a graphics programmer. I became interested in CG at the beginning of 2023 and so, I started learning OpenGL by writing a small 3D engine from scratch. Later, through university courses in Computer Graphics and Virtual Reality Systems, I deepened my understanding — though I remain mostly self-taught through small personal projects about topics that pique my interest. Right now, my main project is Bjorn, a C++ game engine built on Vulkan. I'm mostly focusing on the renderer side, since it's the part I'm most interested in, but I plan to add an audio system and a basic UI in the future as well.


Outside of graphics programming I enjoy filmmaking, making music, game development and learning languages — mostly Norwegian nowadays, though I also spent about three years learning Japanese on and off. I've also recently gotten into photography, both digital and film.
